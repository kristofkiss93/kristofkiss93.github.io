Explore the Three Main Categories of Electric Vehicles

From compact urban rides to high-performance machines, electric vehicles come in all shapes and styles. Below, we take a closer look at three main categories shaping the future of sustainable driving.

City Electric Vehicles

  • Short-distance commuting, compact size, easy to park
  • Low consumption, smaller battery (~20 - 40 kWh)
  • Typical range: 100 - 250 km (60 - 155 miles).
  • Examples: Smart EQ ForTwo, Dacia Spring

General-Purpose Electric Vehicles

  • Versatile daily use - city + highway driving
  • Larger batteries (~50 - 90 kWh) extended range (185 - 310+ miles)
  • Variety of body types: sedan, hatchback, SUV, crossove
  • Examples: Tesla Model 3 / Model Y, Volkswagen ID.4

Sports and Premium Electric Vehicles

  • Extreme acceleration (0 - 60 mph in under 2 seconds)
  • Very large batteries (~90 - 120+ kWh)
  • Premium interiors, high-end materials and technology
  • Examples: Rimac Nevera, Porsche Taycan Turbo S
